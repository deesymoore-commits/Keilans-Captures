About Me

The Durango & Silverton Narrow Gauge Railroad is a historic steam train that travels through scenic mountain landscapes, offering breathtaking views and a unique experience of Colorado's rich railroad history.

My name is Keilan, I am the owner of this site as well as a manager for a team of photographers at D&SNGRR. I started becoming interested in photography when I started working at the railroad and was taught basic photography skills. Now I currently run the company site and my own team. About a year into my work there, I purchased a personal camera to play around with. This is when I started getting into photography. This page will highlight some recent captures, as well as photos I have taken along the journey.
